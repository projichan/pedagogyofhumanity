Research

The research basis that informs the development of the Pedagogy of Humanity, as well as articles of interest for families, teachers, and community members.

Evidence Base

  • Trauma, stress, and learning (regulate → relate → reason)
  • Ecological systems (Bronfenbrenner) and whole-person needs (Maslow)
  • Protective factors, resilience, and family engagement
  • Trauma-responsive practice in early childhood and K–12 settings

What You’ll Find Here

  • Practice briefs: quick guides for classrooms and programs
  • Family resources: “what this means at home” explainers
