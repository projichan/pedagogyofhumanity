Services for Families

Clarity, calm, and advocacy you can trust. We help families navigate home school systems through a resilience-focused lens.

For a limited time, introductory pricing on all family services starts at $40!

Regular rates: $75 per one hour session.

  • 15-Minute Consult (Free)
  • IEP Review
  • Meeting Support
  • Classroom Behavior Strategies
  • Family Coaching
  • Holistic Family Support

15-Minute Consult: Free

  • For: Families, caregivers, foster or grandfamilies navigating school systems
  • Focus: Explore needs, clarify what services are/aren’t, and outline next steps
  • Outcome: Clarity about options—even if you don’t book

IEP Review

  • Format: Document review with written or verbal recommendations
  • Outcome: Families understand the plan, identify gaps, and feel confident walking into meetings

Meeting Support

  • Format: Virtual accompaniment for IEPs or school meetings.
  • Outcome: Families feel supported and heard; schools experience stronger collaboration

Classroom Behavior Strategies

  • Format: 1:1 session with caregivers
  • Outcome: Trauma-responsive tools to reduce stress at home and align with classroom strategies

Family Coaching

  • Format: Ongoing sessions (individual or bundled)
  • Outcome: Caregivers build confidence, communication skills, and advocacy strategies

Holistic Family Support

  • Format: Coaching + relational strategy sessions
  • Outcome: Strengthened relational safety within the family, consistency between home and school, reduced stress
