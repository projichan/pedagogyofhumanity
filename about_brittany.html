About Brittany

Brittany Stratton is the creator of the Pedagogy of Humanity, a framework rooted in love, dignity, and safety as the foundation of all learning. Drawing on professional expertise in early childhood and special education, combined with lived experience of chronic illness and caregiving, Brittany works to bring compassion and humanity into educational and community spaces. Her guiding principle: safety is the highest form of love.
