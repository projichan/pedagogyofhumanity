Services for Organizations

Build trauma-responsive systems that are humane, effective, and sustainable.

  • 15-Minute Consult (Free)
  • Strategy Session
  • Policy & Culture Review
  • Curriculum & Program Design
  • Partnership & Training
  • Speaking & Keynotes

15-Minute Consult: Free

  • For: Schools, universities, or organizations curious about trauma-responsive education
  • Focus: Identify your top challenge, introduce our approach, outline possible next steps
  • Outcome: Clarity about how we can help + invitation into a paid engagement

Strategy Session

  • Format: 90-minute virtual consultation
  • Investment: $225
  • Outcome: Clear, research-aligned recommendations and immediate next steps

Policy & Culture Review

  • Format: Review of institutional policies, handbooks, and culture practices with written recommendations
  • Investment: $2K–$5K (depending on scope and size of institution)
  • Outcome: Actionable strategies to align policies and institutional culture with trauma-responsive principles

Curriculum & Program Design

  • Format: Project (4–8 weeks)
  • Investment: $3K–$10K, depending on scope
  • Outcome: Trauma-responsive curriculum modules for undergrad teacher-prep programs

Partnership & Training

  • Format: Ongoing consulting (retainer or multi-day workshops)
  • Investment: $5K+/month or custom pricing
  • Outcome: Long-term systemic transformation across schools or programs

Speaking & Keynotes

  • Format: Conferences, PD days, panels
  • Investment: Starting at $3K (depends on length/audience/location)
  • Outcome: Inspire, educate, and position your institution at the forefront of trauma-responsive education
